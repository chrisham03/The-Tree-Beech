ABOUT

WHAT WE DO

Here at The Tree Beech, we are committed to providing anyone, from an experienced landscaper to someone just discovering their green thumb, access to sustainably sourced tree saplings. Since 2007 we have grown across the country and have greatly expanded the number of physical locations and nursuries that we offer.

LEARN MORE

HOURS OF OPERATION

The Tree Beech products are available to purchase 24/7! However, standard shipping rates will apply and we will strive to have your orders arrive as quickly as possible.

PHYSICAL STORE HOURS

All of our physical stores are open from 10AM to 6PM local timezones Monday-Friday.

LOCATIONS

The Tree Beech is primarily online, but we do have several physical stores located across the country that provide direct services. There are The Tree Beech locations in:

  • Boston, Massachusetts
  • Chicago, Illinois
  • Laurel, Mississippi
  • Big Sur, California
  • Griffin, Georgia
  • Spokane, Washington

We also partner with local tree nurseries to provide you with quick tree care services.
