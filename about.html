MEET OUR FOUNDERS

ELIZABETH BELLINGTON

Born and raised in Kansas City, Kansas, Elizabeth grew up loving the trees in her backyard and wanted to work with trees in the future. She met the her fellow founders of The Tree Beech when she attended Southern New Hampshire University, where she earned her degree in Environmental Science.

CHRISTINE WILLIAMS

Born and raised in New York City, New York, Christine always wanted to learn more about nature. Her parents worked for the New York State Department of Environmental Conservation, where she anticipated working in the future. However, after meeting her fellow founders of The Tree Beech while attending Southren New Hampshire University for a degree in Environmental Science, she decided to create The Tree Beech with them instead.

MICHAEL SMITH

Born and raised in Concorde, New Hampshire, Michael was constantly surrounded by nature, and particularly trees. In highschool, Michael participated in a program where he and his classmates planted trees. It was this program that made him persue a degree in Environmental Science at Southern New Hampshire University.

WHAT WE DO

Here at The Tree Beech, we are committed to providing anyone, from an experienced landscaper to someone just discovering their green thumb, access to sustainably sourced tree saplings. Since 2007 we have grown across the country and have greatly expanded the number of physical locations and nursuries that we offer.

HOURS OF OPERATION

The Tree Beech products are available to purchase 24/7! However, standard shipping rates will apply and we will strive to have your orders arrive as quickly as possible.

PHYSICAL STORE HOURS

All of our physical stores are open from 10AM to 6PM local timezones Monday-Friday.

LOCATIONS

The Tree Beech is primarily online, but we do have several physical stores located across the country that provide direct services. There are The Tree Beech locations in:

  • Boston, Massachusetts
  • Chicago, Illinois
  • Laurel, Mississippi
  • Big Sur, California
  • Griffin, Georgia
  • Spokane, Washington

We also partner with local tree nurseries to provide you with quick tree care services.
